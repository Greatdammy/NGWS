Equipping
the next

The NextGen Wealth Summit (NGWS) is The Christian Brethren Church’s flagship gathering for working professionals aged 25–45 who are serious about building kingdom-aligned financial futures.

NGWS 2026 brings together seasoned practitioners to equip the next generation with the tools, mindset, and spiritual grounding to steward wealth with purpose.

This is a free, full-day event. All you need is a registered seat and a hunger to grow. Capacity is strictly 120 — secure your seat early.

04 · Frequently asked

Questions
answered.

Is the event free to attend?+
Yes — completely free. NGWS 2026 is a gift from The Christian Brethren Church to the community. Simply register your seat online. No payment required at any stage.
Who is this event for?+
NGWS is designed for working professionals aged 25–45 who want to grow in financial intelligence while staying rooted in their faith. However, anyone with a hunger to learn is welcome.
Do I need to print my ticket?+
No — you can present your ticket digitally on your phone or print it. Either works at the door. Your unique QR code will be scanned for check-in.
Where exactly is the venue?+
The event holds at the TCBC Auditorium. Full address and directions will be sent in your registration confirmation email. Parking is available on-site.
What should I bring?+
Your ticket (phone or print), a valid ID, and a notebook — there will be a lot to write down. Lunch is provided. Smart-casual dress is recommended.
How many seats are available?+
120 seats only. Allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Registration closes once capacity is reached — do not wait.
