01 · About the summit

Financial
Freedom for
Generation
Next.

The NextGen Wealth Summit (NGWS) is The Afolabi Oladele Legacy Foundation’s flagship gathering for young people. If you are a working professional building a career, an entrepreneur, or a student still finding your footing with a strong desire to create a kingdom-aligned financial future,thenthis is for you.

NGWS 2026 features a seasoned practitioner who will equip you with the tools, mindset, and spiritual grounding to steward wealth with purpose — meeting you exactly where you are, whether in the boardroom or the classroom.

This is a free, One-day event.
All you need is a registered seat and a hunger to grow.
Capacity is strictly 120 — secure your seat early.

04 · Frequently asked

Questions
answered.

Is the event free to attend?+
Yes — completely free. NGWS 2026 is a gift from The Afolabi Oladele Legacy Foundation to YOU. Simply register to secure your seat. No payment required at any stage.
Do I need to print my ticket?+
Not really — you can present your ticket digitally on your phone or print it. Either works at the door. Your unique QR code will be scanned for check-in.
Where exactly is the venue?+
The event holds at the TCBC Youth Church, 16 Brethren Ave, Ejigbo, Lagos. Parking is available on-site.
What should I bring?+
Your ticket (phone or print) and a writting material — there will be a lot to write down. Smart casual outfit recommended.
How many seats are available?+
120 seats only. Allocated on a first-come, first-served basis. Registration closes once capacity is reached — do not wait.
